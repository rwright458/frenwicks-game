# **Ace**

![markup_1000010655.png|300](/img/user/Images/markup_1000010655.png)

### **Quick Snapshot**
- Human Bard, College of Lore
- Pronouns: they/them
- Height: 5'10"

### **Backstory**
Ace is a nonbinary human bard who was kidnapped as a baby grew up in the Feywild. They were originally stolen by the Unseelie court, but somehow ended up in the Seelie court, but they were too young to remember the details of how that came to be. They were a servant within the Seelie court, acting as a companion and whipping boy to the heir to the noble house they served in. As a teenager, Ace helps to thwart an assassination attempt against the house's heir. During the attempt, the heir was injured, and Ace healed him. As a result, the nobleman of the house granted Ace their freedom and helped them disguise themself to hide their human identity. 

Ace then joined a travelling circus to look for information about how to leave the Feywild and return to the Material Plane to look for information about their biological family. In the circus, Ace became a skilled bard. Their adventures in the circus also connected them with [[Character Profile -- Ash\|Ash]], their best friend. Ace and Ash travel to Frenwick's Game together. Ace has heard tell of the great riches and treasures that one can earn by winning, and wants to obtain resources to fund their search for their family in the Material Plane.

### **Personality traits:**
- Kind of vintictive
- LOVES shiny things
- Wants more than anything to belong somewhere and have a family, found or otherwise
